BadBank

This is a front end project that I did for MIT xPRO. With it, I demonstrated my knowledge of how to use react, bootstrap, carrying of context and state and general themes of front end development. As the name suggests, this is a "bad bank" because it stores your data without a back end for all the world to see. That was our professor's sense of humor, with the task of making it into a better banking app for the full stack capstone project.
